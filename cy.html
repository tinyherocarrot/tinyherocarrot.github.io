COOPERATIVELY YOURS -- UI/UX Design for Mobile
For a Design Methods class I created a mobile app UI for the Berkeley Student Cooperative webpage. I also integrated the recently-introduced Member Portal, where BSC members make rent payments and housing selections electronically. I drew on the original, earthtone color scheme of the BSC webpage. The prototype demonstrates an interface for both current and prospective BSC members to conveniently access important information and resources from their mobile device.
