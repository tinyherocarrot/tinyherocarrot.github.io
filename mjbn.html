KINGMAN HALL BAND NIGHT POSTER
Informational poster designed for UC Berkeley SERC's (Student Environmental Resource Center) 2016 Gala event. SERC is a resource hub for sustainability efforts and organizations on campus, providing educational programming, leadership development, financial resources and more. The 2016 Gala celebrated a successful sustainability referendum called The Green Initiative Fund (TGIF), which will raise more than $3 million for sustainability projects over the next 10 years.

In the spring of 2016, I worked at SERC as Marketing Associate, designing branding and logos for various sustainability groups on campus, including campaign materials for TGIF. Photo credits to Katherine Walsh.
