PROJECTOR ROOM PLAY READING ENSEMBLE

Logo design my friend Ely asked me to do for his newly-formed indie theatre troupe. "Set in a projector room, PRPR Ensemble hosts bi-monthly play readings of queer and multiethnic narrative." I used a motif of a colored handprint with a queer or gender-queer symbol inscribed on the palm to represent an intersectional identity. Together, the handprints form a speech bubble: the collective voice. READ MORE

Typefaces: Tw Cen MT, Orator Std
